Rebecca F. Kuang

Award Winning Novelist

R.F. Kuang is a best-selling novelist known for her thought-provoking works that blend history, fantasy, and sharp social commentary. She gained widespread recognition with The Poppy War trilogy and further cemented her literary success with Babel and Yellowface. Her writing explores themes of power, identity, and the consequences of history, earning her numerous awards and critical acclaim.
